About propertyVR

propertyVR's purpose is to allow clients to view or inspect a property they are either looking to purchase or are building from scratch. This will create a new opportunity for real estate agencies to conduct inspections for clients purchasing properties which might be at a distance from their current location.

Along with that it will allow clients to view their property before itâ€™s even made if they are constructing a property. People building their own new property or home from scratch are able to send their floor plan to us where a model of their property will be made in which they will be allowed to view before finalising and getting the property to be constructed. Real estate agencies will be able to receive models of properties they are selling and display them to clients wishing to purchase while not having to physically be at the property.
